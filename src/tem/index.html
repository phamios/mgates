Send Mass Payments to your Payees Worldwide

Get Started
Need to get paid by companies in the EU, UK, China or Japan?

In today’s borderless world, having the ability to easily send payments to multiple users at once, worldwide, is vital. Payoneer’s innovative payment platform enables global companies and marketplaces to transfer funds quickly, securely and at reduced cost to more than 200 countries and regions in over 150 currencies.
Users are given the flexibility of choosing how to receive their funds, by the method that best suits them.
